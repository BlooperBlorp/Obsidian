A mercantile group with a deep history rooted in arcane arts. While in the modern day they operate just as a normal mercantile group, some of the brotherhood is steeped in tradition. Primarily, high-ranking officials in the group are required to take up wizardry.

As a high ranking mercantile group, that also deals in the arcane arts, that was ALSO in close proximity to the [[gash in the weave]], they are the primary established mercantile group stationed in [[Aeglirin]]

While the entire group is interested in seeing profits from collecting and selling [[Weave infused materials]], some of the higher ups also have other ideas...
